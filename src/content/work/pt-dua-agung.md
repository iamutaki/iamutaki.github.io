PT Dua Agung is a building-materials distribution company whose main products include Semen Gresik, Nusa Board, and Gyproc. To support its operations and stand out as a leading distributor, the company also provides expedition, loading, delivery, land-clearing, heavy-equipment rental, and building-materials trading services.

The company develops an Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system — specialized for cement distribution — that helps manage stock, process orders, and compile financial reports more effectively.

**Tech stack:** CodeIgniter, Vue.js, Flutter, Laravel, MySQL, PostgreSQL, Struts, PHP
