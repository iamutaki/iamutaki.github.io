An app designed to help people learn Sambas-language vocabulary and encourage everyone to contribute by suggesting words that may be unfamiliar to younger generations. The goal is to promote the Sambas language nationally, inviting community members who know local terms to help expand the dictionary into a shared, useful resource for all.
